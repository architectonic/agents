# Brazilian Tax Law Knowledge Set

This is a specialization attachment template, not a legal corpus.

A local law/accounting/tax organization may attach:

```text
knowledge/jurisdictions/br/legal-code/
knowledge/jurisdictions/br/tax/
knowledge/clients/<client>/
projects/<matter>/
```

## Requirements

- Use authoritative sources only for law text and official changes.
- Record effective dates and source URLs or local source IDs.
- Mark obsolete law, revoked rules, and uncertain interpretations.
- Require human/legal review before client-facing conclusions.
- Maintain through `living-knowledge` campaigns when the corpus changes frequently.
